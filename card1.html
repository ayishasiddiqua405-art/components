Facebook

Community and Diverse Formats Facebook is the most versatile platform, supporting nearly every content type for a broad global audience. Groups & Community: A primary hub for niche interest groups (e.g., cooking, local news, hobbyist forums). Mixed Media: High engagement on static images, long-form text (informative/how-to), and Live broadcasts. AI Discovery: In 2025, up to 50% of the feed comes from "unconnected sources" based on AI-predicted interests.

YouTube

2.53 billion to 2.58 billion users As a video-first platform and the world’s second-largest search engine, YouTube leads in user time spent and is the primary destination for long-form educational and entertainment content.

Instagram

3.0 billion users (estimated) Highly visual and popular among Gen Z and Millennials, Instagram focuses on storytelling through photos, Stories, and Reels. Some reports now place its user base neck-and-neck with WhatsApp at the 3 billion mark.
